Who We Are

Layman Saloon - Pune’s Most Relaxing Hair Saloon for Men

Layman Saloon brings the latest trends in men’s hair from classic barbering to contemporary hairdressing.
An award winning team of experienced stylists who are passionate in giving their clients an experience to remember, making sure they leave the salon looking better than ever.
We know that busy urban men want the ease of themselves being able to visit one brilliant hairdresser, rather than try to accommodate 3 different saloons 3 different times.

We offer all the creative, precise and a high standard of haircutting in a friendly relaxed environment that is tailored to stylish men, with the best treats in town!
We will never rush a haircut, our goal is to create a timeless experience that is as seamless and enjoyable as possible with a focus on excellent results for your hair.
